Jackie's Guide to Making a Website Zine

$20

Free shipping currently to U.S. only.

Want to make your own website, but don’t know where to start? Do you wonder how to better tell your story and create a space on the web that’s uniquely you? This is the comic for you!

Jackie’s Guide to Making a Website (2020) is a 28-page, Risograph-printed comic guide to help you brainstorm, design, and bring a personal website to life. This digital zine includes personal anecdotes, hands-on design exercises, and resources around making a website aligned with your goals and identity.

You will explore:

  • How to introspect and refine your own self-story
  • How to prototype a website that fits your needs
  • Other tools and resources for bring your website to life

Developed originally at SVA RisoLAB in 2019 and reprinted with Risolve Studio in 2020.

Paperback; 5 1/2 x 7 1/2; 28 pages.

Also available as a digital download on itch.io!
