7 minutes in heaven

A website for two people to be together at once.

View site

7 minutes in heaven is a multiplayer website that lets only two people be together at once, for seven minutes at a time.

This work was part of People of the Labyrinth: LEISURE CENTRE at Public Works Administration in May/June 2024. The piece was displayed as an installation of two computers connected to same website placed on either end of the gallery, creating a heavenly portal to be with other visitors in the gallery.

Photography by Courtney Kinnare
