Making a Website (by you and for you)

Summer 2019 · Comic guidebook

About

In Summer 2019, I decided to sign up to teach a class for BUFU's WYFY School on using website design to tell your personal story. I myself also decided to attend a summer class at SVA's RisoLab on developing and printing mini-comics. The result is a 28-page comic called Jackie's Guide to Making a Website: by you & for you, made in a labor-of-love.
