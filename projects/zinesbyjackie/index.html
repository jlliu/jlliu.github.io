Zines by Jackie

Fall 2020 · Online shop / Curriculum

Zines by Jackie is an in-development hub for zines, activities, and resources about technology, art, design, and more.

Inspired by the positive reception of my Making a Website Zine, I'm currently working to create a larger zine series on topics related to technology, with each zine complemented by online resources and activities.

The Zines by Jackie website will function as an online shop to purchase Risograph printed zines, as well as a center for digital learning resources - such as design brainstorming activities, resource compilations, and beginner-friendly starter code.

Combining what I've learned across the tech, design, and art worlds, Zines by Jackie is my personal response to seeing a need for more holistic, socially-centered ways of learning about, and with, technology. Check back soon (~October 2020) to buy zines and explore activities when Zines by Jackie launches!
