about me

i am an artist-at-heart, a taurus, and a master's student at NYU's Interactive Telecommunications Program, graduating spring 2020. previously, i have worked as a product designer at Codecademy and Girls Who Code, and studied computer science at MIT.

i tend to shift between various roles of artist/designer/technologist - but currently, i'm particularly excited by how art and design can be modes of intervention into dominant narratives of computing technology.

as a designer who's worked in CS education, i'm interested in ways to transform gate-keeped fields, like programming, to be more accessible, inclusive, and human.

as an artist, i'm interested in co-opting technological processes to carve space for radical vulnerability and sensitivity in a world that does not traditionally bring justice to these values, all within a speculative, research-based practice.

i'm open to opportunities for work, artistic collaboration, or having a coffee or bubble tea. don't be afraid to get in touch!
