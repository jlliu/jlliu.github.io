about me

i am an artist-at-heart, a taurus, and a master's student at NYU's Interactive Telecommunications Program, graduating spring 2020. previously, i worked as a product designer at Codecademy and Girls Who Code, and studied computer science at MIT.

i'm particularly interested in the potential for design and art to interrogate computing culture and radically reimagine how it can be more equitable.

as a designer who's worked in ed-tech, i worked to transform gate-keeped fields, like programming, to be more accessible, inclusive, and human.

as an artist, i'm interested in co-opting technological processes to carve a space for radical vulnerability and sensitivity in a world that does not traditionally bring justice to these values.

i'm open to part-time, contract, and full-time work after graduating. please send me a message if you'd like to collaborate!
