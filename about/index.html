Hi, I'm Jackie!

I'm an artist and digital designer who creates playful, narrative experiences with (and about) technology.

I grew up alongside the 2000s internet, and draw from it in autobiographical work that blends websites, interface design, visual narrative, and games. I'm interested in nostalgia as a forward-thinking practice, and my work remembers and reimagines technology to experiment with ways of relating–with myself, with others, and with technology systems.

I’m also a digital product designer with 6+ years of experience focused in STEM education and early-stage startups. My ideal future working contexts are those that value play, connection, and liberation.

I’m open to artistic/design collaborations, teaching workshops and speaking about my practice, and making new connections. If this resonates with you, let's get in touch!
