Product Design consulting

Are you an small startup or organization looking for design help? Need a UX audit of your product with next steps, your very first design system, or just an extra hand reviewing portfolios while hiring? Let's talk!

Since 2021, I've helped 2 early-stage startups go from scrappy MVPs to full-fledged web platforms as a solo/founding designer in tiny teams. I know how to sift through ambiguity to get to the big picture, address complex problems at their roots, balance vision with pragmatism, and make a lot of magic happen with just a little.

Reach out if...

  • Your brand centers play, creativity, learning, community.
  • You're ready to invest in design work that'll elevate your product–but don’t yet need a full-time designer.
  • You're looking for a time-boxed contract and/or part-time help.

Previous work: Codédex, Processing Foundation, Schoolhouse.world, Codecademy, Girls Who Code

Interactive web experiences

I've been designing and developing playful, experimental websites for over 10 years, and got pretty good at it! Reach out to me if you're an artist who wants a unique web presence, a small brand that wants a custom dressup game, or something else!

Reach out if...

  • You're looking for something fun, interactive, and in my design / illustration style.
  • You want an experience that feels like a Flash-era website from 2005, but with the sensibilities of the 2025 web.
  • You're tired of generic, corporatized web design and want an experience that's refreshing, heartfelt, and handmade.

Art shows & commissions
